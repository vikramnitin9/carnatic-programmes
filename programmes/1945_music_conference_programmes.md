## Friday 28th December 1945 at 5:30 p.m.

**Bai Roshanara Begum and Party** — Hindustani Vocal

| Raga | Tempo |
|------|-------|
| Bhim Pilas | Bilambat and Jalad |
| Shudh Sarang | do |
| Thumri — Piloo | |

> **Bhim Pilas**: Oudav Sampuran; corresponding to Abheri as a janya of Kharaharapriya, 22nd mela. Khyal in slow and quick tempo.
>
> **Shudh Sarang**: Oudav; of the 22nd mela but also takes Guta (or preti) Madhyama. Khyal in slow and quick tempo.
>
> **Piloo**: Sampuran; primarily of the notes of Kiravani, 21st mela with a mixture of Jhanjuti as of 28th mela. Thumri is a type of composition which admits of shades of other melodies.

## Sunday 30th December 1945 at 3:30 p.m.

**Bai Roshana Begum and Party** — Hindustani Vocal

| Raga | Tempo |
|------|-------|
| Bhopali | Bilambat and Jalad |
| Hamsudni | do |
| Thumri — Jhanjhoti | |

> **Bhoopali**: Oudav; corresponding to Mohana wherein the notes are all tivra or sharp. Khyal in slow and quick tempo.
>
> **Hamsudni**: Oudav; corresponding to Hamsadhvani of the 29th scale, Sankarabharana. Khyal in slow and quick tempo.
>
> **Jhanjhoti**: Shadav Sampuran; corresponding to Kambhoji of 28th mela. Thumri admits mixtures of other ragas.
